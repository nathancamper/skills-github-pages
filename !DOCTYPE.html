Scope of Work & Deliverables

This section details the specific services, tasks, and tangible outcomes of the project. Clearly defining the scope and deliverables ensures mutual understanding and sets expectations for the project's execution and completion.

Project Goal:

To complete comprehensive asphalt demolition, excavation, hauling, and new surface installation, alongside detailed electrical work, to facilitate an upgraded electrical service at the Ourisman Chevrolet of Marlow Heights location.

Tasks Included:

  • All labor, material, and supervision to complete the scope of work including:
  • Asphalt Demolition
    • Saw cut all lines
    • Demolish - (56 sqft.) Primary Line, (25 sqft.) Secondary Line, (225 sqft.) Transformer Location
  • Excavation
    • Excavation to be conducted using machinery and hand digging.
    • Excavate - (7 YDS) Primary Line, (3.5 YDS) Secondary Line, (1 YD) Transformer Location, (4 YDS) Utility Bollards, Halo Grounding Ring excavation provision.
  • Hauling
    • Remove excess spoils by means of hauling away
    • Haul (11 YDS) Soil, (6 YDS) Asphalt
  • New Gravel
    • Install new gravel according to plan
    • (2.5 tons) CR6 for trench lines, (6.5 tons) CR6 for transformer pad
  • New Concrete
    • Primary duct bank flow-able fill (2 YDS)
    • Secondary duct bank flow-able fill (1.5 YDS)
    • Utility Bollards 3000 PSI (4.5 YDS)
  • New Asphalt
    • Primary Asphalt (2 ton top)
    • Secondary Asphalt (1 ton top)
    • Transformer Area Asphalt (6.5 tons top)
  • Electrical
    • Underground PVC per plan
    • Grounding per plan including halo grounding ring
    • Secondary terminations as required
    • EV Charging Equipment terminations
  • Equipment
    • Mini Excavator w/ 12" bucket
    • Hydraulic Hammer
    • Tracked Skid Steer
    • Plate Tamper
    • Dump Trailer
    • Saw Cutter
    • Haul Trucks
  • Miscellaneous
    • (10) Utility Bollards
    • Pre-cast transformer pad
    • (10) Bollard Covers

Deliverables:

  • Completed site preparation, including asphalt demolition, excavation, hauling, new gravel, concrete, and asphalt installation as specified.
  • Fully installed underground PVC and grounding system, including halo grounding ring.
  • Completed secondary and EV Charging Equipment terminations.
  • Installation of 10 utility bollards and one pre-cast transformer pad.
  • [Any additional specific deliverables, e.g., project documentation, final inspection]
